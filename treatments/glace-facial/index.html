Glacē Facial (Korean Glass Skin Treatment)

The Glacē facial is a multi-step treatment designed to cleanse, hydrate, and deeply exfoliate the skin, delivering the smooth, luminous "glass skin" look the treatment is known for, with zero downtime.

Benefits

  • Deep hydration and exfoliation
  • Helps clear mild acne, blackheads, and whiteheads
  • Smoother, firmer, and more hydrated-looking skin
  • Improves the appearance of fine lines and pigmentation

What the Treatment Includes

  • Microdermabrasion with serum infusion for deep exfoliation
  • Dual cupping to boost lymphatic drainage of the face
  • Facial massage
  • Targeted serum infusion, customized to your skin's specific needs
  • Hydrating, soothing mask for an added glow
  • Customized LED therapy

What Makes It Different

Unlike more intensive treatments, Glacē is designed to be gentle enough for regular use, with many clients seeing visible results after their very first session.

Who Is a Candidate

Glacē is well-suited for most skin types, including sensitive and acne-prone skin. As with any facial treatment, clients with active skin infections, open lesions, active cystic acne, recent sunburn, or specific serum allergies should consult with the team.

After Your Treatment

  • Wear broad-spectrum SPF 30+ immediately after treatment and every day going forward
  • Apply a gentle moisturizer to help maintain hydration and results
  • Avoid exfoliants, scrubs, harsh irritants, and waxing for 5–7 days post-treatment

Treatment Schedule

Monthly maintenance sessions are recommended for long-lasting results.
