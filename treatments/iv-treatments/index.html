IV Hydration & Wellness Therapy

IV therapy delivers fluids, vitamins, and electrolytes directly into your bloodstream, bypassing digestion for faster absorption than drinking water or taking supplements alone.

Our IV Blends

Recharge Elixir

Rapid hydration with electrolytes and IV fluids, revitalizing and refreshing when you're feeling depleted.

The After Party

A multi-vitamin and B12 recovery drip, formulated to help you feel restored after a big night out.

Wellness Warrior

An immunity-boosting blend of vitamins and antioxidants to cleanse and recharge your body.

Who Should Avoid IV Therapy

  • Kidney or heart conditions
  • Liver disease
  • Uncontrolled high blood pressure
  • Known allergy to any ingredient in the selected blend
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding (confirm with your provider)

What to Expect

Sessions typically take 30–45 minutes. Most clients feel refreshed shortly after treatment. Mild bruising or tenderness at the IV site is possible and usually resolves within a day or two.

Treatment Schedule

Use IV therapy as needed. Some clients book around a specific event or illness, others come in monthly as part of an ongoing wellness routine.
