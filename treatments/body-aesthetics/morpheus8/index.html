RF Microneedling (Morpheus8)

Morpheus8 is a nonsurgical treatment that combines microneedling with fractional radiofrequency (RF) to resurface the skin and rebuild collagen deeper down. Fine needles create tiny channels in the skin while delivering RF energy to deeper layers, stimulating new collagen and tightening existing collagen fibers. Because the treatment is fractional, it affects only a portion of the skin at a time, allowing for faster healing and less downtime than more aggressive resurfacing options.

Treatable Concerns

  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Pigmentation and sun damage
  • Skin laxity and sagging
  • Uneven skin tone and texture
  • Acne scarring and other scar types
  • Enlarged pores
  • Stretch marks
  • Jowls and lack of jawline definition
  • Crepey skin
  • Submental fat (double chin)
  • Collagen depletion

How It Works

  • The treatment area is numbed with a topical anesthetic
  • Fine needles create microchannels while delivering RF energy to a customized depth
  • Needle depth and RF intensity are adjusted based on the area and each client's specific concerns

Treatment Summary

  • Anesthetic: Topical or local
  • Procedure time: 30–60 minutes
  • Downtime: 1–5 days
  • Full results: 4–6 weeks
  • Longevity: 12–18 months

Before Treatment

  • Follow your provider's guidance on adjusting skincare products, medications, supplements, or alcohol beforehand
  • Minimize sun exposure and prioritize hydration in the days leading up to treatment

Who Should Avoid Morpheus8

  • Active acne, rosacea, or other active inflammatory skin conditions
  • Open wounds or active infection
  • Active herpes simplex (cold sore) outbreak
  • Pregnancy
  • Known metal or skin allergies
  • Recent Botox, filler, or other injectable treatments in the area (within the past 6 months)
  • Recent facelift, eyelid surgery, deep chemical peel, or other procedures on the treatment area (within the past 3–12 months, depending on procedure)
  • Sunburned skin within the past 2 weeks
  • Use of oral retinoids or certain autoimmune therapies (your provider will advise on timing)

After Treatment

  • Redness, swelling, or sensitivity is expected and typically resolves within a few days
  • Full collagen remodeling and results build gradually over several weeks

A Note on Safety

In 2025, the FDA issued safety communication about RF microneedling devices, highlighting the importance of proper training and technique. Morpheus8 remains safe and effective when performed by trained, qualified medical providers.
