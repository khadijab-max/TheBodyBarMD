Forma (InMode)

Collagen and elastin are the structural proteins that keep skin firm, supple, and smooth. As we age, our bodies naturally produce less of both, leading to looser, less defined skin and more visible fine lines.

Forma is a noninvasive, non-surgical treatment for tightening and lifting the skin on the face and neck, one of the InMode treatments that has become a client favorite.

How It Works

Forma uses controlled radiofrequency (RF) energy to heat the dermis to approximately 40°C. A water-based gel helps the treatment probe glide smoothly across the skin during the procedure. This gentle, sustained heating stimulates collagen induction, helping rebuild the skin's structural support from within. The result is a firmer, more lifted, and more defined appearance over time.

Benefits

  • Tightens and lifts skin on the face and neck
  • Defines the jawline
  • Improves cheek contour for a more sculpted appearance
  • Mild, gradual skin tightening and lifting
  • Noninvasive, with little to no downtime

Who Should Avoid Forma

  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Metal implants, plates, screws, or silicone implants in the treatment area
  • Current or history of skin cancer, or pre-malignant moles
  • Impaired immune system
  • Active skin conditions in the treatment area (open sores, psoriasis, eczema, rash, infection)
  • Isotretinoin (Accutane) use within the past 6 months
  • History of keloid scarring, or very dry/fragile skin
  • History of melasma or other pigmentation-prone conditions
  • Recently tanned skin, natural or sunless (avoid tanning for 2–4 weeks before treatment)

What to Expect After Treatment

Mild redness is common and typically settles within 1–2 days.

Treatment Schedule

Most clients see optimal results with a series of 6–8 sessions, spaced about one week apart, followed by periodic maintenance sessions (typically every few months) to sustain results. Your provider will tailor the exact schedule to your skin and goals during your consultation.
